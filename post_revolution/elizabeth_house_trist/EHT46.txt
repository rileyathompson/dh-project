anderson and colonel lewis of virginia and a mr trant a young gentleman lately from ireland and has lately open a store at this place we leave the falls all matter right in our boat the day too warm to be agreeable and what wind there is against us the river appear to more advantage this side the falls then above the shore seem clean and in many place for about a mile in  length there is a regular wall to support the bank as if build of the first hewn stone one of our men went on shore to hunt and being gone several hour return with a deer the first wild meat we have had sun set pass salt river which run up in the kentucky settlement as the are call i am inform there are a number of good plantation up on this river at ten
