afraid of them i dare hardly stay in the house one night much less over the sabbath i cannot write so good night i have hesitate a long time whether i ought to write or not and have at length conclude i may as well write as anything else for i cannot read or listen to deacon w who is read for i am almost distracted we have determine or rather mr w has and we must do as he say to spend the sabbath among these wicked wretch it would not be against my conscience to ride today rather than stay here for we can do no good and get none and how much harm they may do us i know not but they look as if they had sufficient inclination to do us evil i can wait no long to write you for i have a great deal
