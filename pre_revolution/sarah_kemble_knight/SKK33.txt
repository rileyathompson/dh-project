two a clock afternoon we arrive at newhaven where i was receive with all possible respect and civility here i discharge mr wheeler with a reward to his satisfaction and take some time to rest after so long and toilsome a journey and inform myself of the manner and custom of the place and at the same time employ myself in the affair i went there upon they are govern by the same law as we in boston or little differ throughout this whole colony of connecticut and much the same way of church government and many of them good sociable people and i hope religion too but a little too much independent in their principle and as i have been tell were former in their zeal very rigid in their administration toward such as their law make offender even to a harmless kiss or innocent merriment among young
