to stay a few week longer harriet howards expect me a week in cambridge mrs sumner a week in boston and mrs hasket derby another week i am now with ellen and shall stay til mrs coffin come up then according to promise go to mrs lucy derby s i feel extreme anxious to hear you have move into town and shall most probably be here until then write me immediate if you wish any furniture mrs sumner will assist me in purchase whatever you wish i mentione in my letter when i set out on this journey i borrow fifteen dollars of ellen i wish you to send it to me immediate after receive this if you have not already send it i shall likewise stand in need of a little as i have unavoidable incur many expense in this journey which i should not otherwise have done
