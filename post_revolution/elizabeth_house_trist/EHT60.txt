mosquito the navigation of this river is render dangerous by a number of log that are fix by one end at the bottom of the river and the other end is about two feet about the water many boat are lose by run upon these log and the great attention is necessary to avoid them however we are happy in being with careful good men well acquaint with the river at dawn of day we leave the shore and soon come in sight of the iron bank a great quantity of ore may be pick up on the surface of the earth it is sixteen mile from the ohio a fine high situation and i am tell there is to be a town lay out here very soon at ten o clock we pass reed creek twelve mile from the iron bank we see some elk on a sandbar
