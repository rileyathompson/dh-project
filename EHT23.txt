still excessive cold our horse scarce able to keep their feet we stop at a little cabin to warm ourselves being almost force ride only five mile after get a little thaw we again proceed on our journey our difficulty seem to increase we were oblige to dismount at every hill or run the risk of break our neck sometimes our horse would tumble and sometimes ourselves i had one tumble that hurt me a good deal descend a very steep hill at the foot of which run turkey creek which is twelve mile from pittsburgh we find the creek so full of ice as to make it at that place impossible for my part i would rather have run the risk than ascend the hill again notwithstanding the man who live on the other side call us not to attempt it unless we mean to lose our live
