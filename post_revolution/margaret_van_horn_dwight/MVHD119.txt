i walk as much as i could we are in a comfortable house before an excellent fire it is snow very fast we set out early with the hope of get to youngstown at night and to warren to night but four mile from youngstown the horse were so tired they would not stir so we stop at a private house for the night an hour before sun down we had been in the house but a little time when susan look out and told me she think there was some one after me and i soon saw mr edwards and two horse i was never so happy i think i run out to meet him he come in and sit a while and just at dark we start for youngstown mr edwards insist upon susan s go with us so she ride behind him and i ride the single
