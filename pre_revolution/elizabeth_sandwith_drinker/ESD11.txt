with my foot rs hd and hannah went to the children meeting this morning they drink tea at timothy horsefields h vanvleck and his wife there rain this day which confine us all within only hd drink tea at oberlins this morning hd went to fish without success in the afternoon i ride to horsefields and drink tea with our company their family hv and wife set our face homeward ride ten mile very bad road to widow teters from thence seven mile of a very rough tedious way to near tohiccon where we dine and after fording the creek ride about fifteen mile to bartholomews which we did not arrive at for near an hour after dark here we lodge and fare well ride eleven mile to yunkins at the foot of chesnutt hill breakfast and then put forward reach home about dinner time
