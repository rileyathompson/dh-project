very busy in get my thing together just before noon hear mr woodbridge is take very ill with a vomit and purge so can t think of go at present i was at a loss what to do but upon consideration and advice conclude to go when the wagon came for perhaps i may loose the opportunity of mr fondas sloop which is the only one that I am certain is go down my father went to mr woodbridge and find him a little better and hope to be able to go before the sloop sail p m five o clock set out in the wagon after an affection parting with all my friend except lucy she ride as far as sheffield and will tarry all night with me part with lucy and set forward with my honest dutchman only it seem very lonesome to go quite
