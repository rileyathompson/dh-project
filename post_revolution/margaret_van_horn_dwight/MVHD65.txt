just at night it begin to rain very fast and i expect we should all catch our death walk through mud and mire with no umbrella or but one that would not cover us all we were wet through before we reach this dreadful place where we now are the woman is cross and the man sick it rain all day yesterday and such a shock place as this is i never saw a dozen wagoner are here some half drunk and no place for us to stay in but our wagon or a little chamber with three square of glass in it with scarce room to sit or stand i am now in despair it continue rain fast than ever the house full of drunk profane wretch the old woman cross as a witch we have nothing to eat and can get nothing but some slapjack at a baker
