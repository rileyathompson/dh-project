i am almost in ecstasy at the magnificent of display of nature the tree are deck in all their gay attire and the earth in its richest verdue so much for bloom may after a good night rest we arise had a comfortable breakfast by nine o clock got as far as the muskingum one hundred seventy one mile from pittsburgh a fine river about three hundred yard wide and about two hundred mile in length take its rise from a swamp about forty mile this side lake erie pass the little kanawha at noon it appear to be about one hundred fifty yard wide along the bank of the river just at that place is a great quantity of very large stone suppose to be of an excellent quality for mill stone equal to the french burr nine o clock in the morning come to the great kanawha
