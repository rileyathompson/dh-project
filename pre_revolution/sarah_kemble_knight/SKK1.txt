about three o clock afternoon i begin my journey from boston to newhaven being about two hundred mile my kinsman captain robert luist wait on me as far as dedham where i was to meet the west post i visit the revere mr belcher the minister of the town and tarry there til evening in hope the post would come along but he not come i resolve to go to billingses where he use to lodge being twelve mile further but being ignorant of the way madam billings see no persuasion of her good spouse or hers could prevail with me to lodge there that night very kind went with me to the tavern where i hope to get my guide and desire the host to inquire of her guest whether any of them would go with me but they being tie by the lip to a pewter engine
