mr and mrs fowler treat us with every possible attention and from the number of stranger resort thither on their way to the cumberland and c make the winter pass a way much more agreeable that it otherwise would we where at several dance at which there would be fifteen or twenty lady and as many gentleman if there had not been those little recreation i should certain have been very miserable the tardy of the boat builder detain us til the river got so low that i was in great trepidation least our passage would be stop but good fortune has attend us three day of rain have swell the river and we have the flatter appearance of a speed voyage our accommodation all thing consider not to be complain off the weather please and everything wear a smile aspect we soon come to mackees island four mile
