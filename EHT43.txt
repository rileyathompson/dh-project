fort where me taylors family reside for the present our accomodation not the most desirable in point of elegance being an old log hut mrs taylor her four daughter polly and myself occupy this place as a chamber we spread our bed at night and in the morning by roll them up they serve as seat an old barrel with a board on the top is our table and this is comfortable to what many are oblige to put up with the polite attention of the gentleman to serve us render our situation as agreeable and more so then we have any reason to expect the fort has been a very good one but is now out of repair there are twelve habitation within the enclosure the town is lay out in lot but not many building erect what there is hardly deserve the name of house but so
