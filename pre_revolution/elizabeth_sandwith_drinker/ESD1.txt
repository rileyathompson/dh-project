leave home after dinner fifth day hd ed and john stop at hoods wait there half an hour for robt and hannah stevenson who accompany us on our tour to lancaster and c bait at the nags head twelve mile from philadelphia come to rowland evanss before seven o clock write to sister this evening sent back my keys which i had unknowingly brought from home sup lodge and breakfast at res road twenty three long mile this day went after breakfast from res his son cadr with us by way of guide foarded schuylkill then went to the yallow spring dine there took a walk to a spring in the meadow hd hs and myself took a duck in the bath set of between three and four from thence hd ed rs hs john and portuga their servant stop a minute at jos
