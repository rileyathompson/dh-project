the limner after meeting we were introduce by anne rodman to her relation of the same name and receive an invitation to dinner it is about four mile and a half out of flushing and a most beauty ride the road round flushing look like please walk of a two chair width fence by low wall with tree plant along them or fine prim hedge this road to rodman s in particular is please by reason of fine rise hill which give a view of the bay and the country clothed at this time in beauty field of grain and pasture there was likewise a fine piece of wood so clear from brush underneath and cover with grass that it seem to invite one to a cool retreat from the noontide ray the house is close by yet within a good distance from the inlet of the bay on
