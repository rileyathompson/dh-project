nine o clock richard smith and anna pole come home from philadelphia bring me a letter of permission from my parents to go in company with anna to newyork and as my relation hear approve of the journey i shall prepare to go anna pole betsey rodman and i went to meeting h smith speak afternoon at home get ready to set out early in the morning i look upon myself in particular under the care of jane burling who was to follow the next stage day because there would have been other way too large a company of us for convenience they were jane burling and her son thomas pryor anne and betsey rodman scaman and thomas rodman richard smith senior thomas powel and his wife anna lodge with me and we rise four o clock dress ourselves by moonlight breakfast with sally set out in the stage
