turn out to be some french men go to the cumberland river to trade their appearance was perfect savage have little or no clothes on and their hide quite as dark as the indian they had come from the opost at nine o clock in the evening we reach as far as the shawanoe on cumberland river it is a large river and run a great way into the country i am inform that it take its rise from the allegheny mountain in washington county virginia the meander of the river make it above two thousand mile to its source we pass the chinokee river in the night a fine breeze but the wind as usual contrary which makes our passage tedious in the morning we come to the massac a place where former there had been a fort and was evacuate by the french in the year fifty
