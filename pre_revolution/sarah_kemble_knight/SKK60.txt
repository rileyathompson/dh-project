to a mill not far off here are three fine tavern within call of each other very good provision for travel thence we travel through merrinak a neat though little place with a navigable river before it one of the please i ever see here were good building especially one a very fine seat which they told me was colonel hethcoats who i had hear was a very fine gentleman from hence we come to horse neck where we bait and they tell me that one church of england parson officiate in all these three town once every sunday in turn throughout the year and that they all could but poorly maintain him which they grudge to do being a poor and quarrel crew as i understand by our host their quarrel about their choice of minister they chose to have none but cause the government to send this
