between eight and nine o clock this morning mrs smith mr clifford and i in the carriage and mr smith on horseback set off for rockypoint about seventeen mile distant the morning was as fine as ever shine in may and the road exceed good we pass through frankfort but see nothing worth remark there save one or two ill done thing and the first house build in the place at present it bear but little the appearance of antique as they have make the outside entire new it is a frame building and small and may be said to resemble the man s knife that had been usefor so long a time only he had had two new blade and as many new handle add the prospect from the hill after cross the bridge is really please one has a fine view of several elegant house on the point
