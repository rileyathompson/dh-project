distant from it mr w went a mile out of his way that we may see the town it contain almost entire dutch people the house there are near all stone but like easton it contain some pretty brick house it has not half as many store as easton the meet house is a curious building it looks like a castle i suppose it is stone the outside is plaster we leave our wagon to view the town we did not know whether the building was a church or the moravian school so we inquire of two or three men who only answer in dutch mr and mrs w were purchase bread and susan and i walk on to inquire we next see a little boy on horseback and he could only say me cannot english but he i believe speak to another for a very pretty boy come near
