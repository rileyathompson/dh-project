we were oblige to go up the hill and ride a mile further up the creek before we could attempt to cross at last we got through but not without being in great danger we for some dinner at at the widow myers and set of in hope to get into pittsburgh before bed time we met with an inhabitant of that place at waltowens pollys horse being in better spirit than mine she push on in company with this man i could not get my horse out of a walk and every step his feet almost slip from under him at last down we come but luck enough to receive no damage only it made his cowardice increase and add nothing to my courage poor beast he tremble every step he take after that night come on and for the first time since i leave home my spirit
