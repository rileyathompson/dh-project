here i am my dear sister once more safe and happy seat at home i have no time to write tomorrow so that this letter must serve for my philadelphia and my brunswick julia and sister i was so complete fatigue this morning that i could not write and now steal an hour that should be devote to repose tomorrow i shall be wholy engage in make provision for my country residence to which i shall repair on wednesday morning our ride from philadelphia to lancaster was horrible fatigue i never experience anything like it and if cry could have done any good i should have follow julia s example and have cry all the way at lancaster we had a miserable dinner which although hunger we could scarce eat it was the same case at supper we sup at the susquehanna julia sleep profound and we near as
