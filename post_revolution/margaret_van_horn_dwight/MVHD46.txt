mere one short dirty street this town is one street only but a tolerable pretty one there are a number of good house in it we have once more got among people of our own nation and language and they appear very clever it has been snow fast all the afternoon and we find it very difficult travel and were oblige to put up just in the edge of the town it was mr w s intention to cross the susquehannah which is the other side the town we shall not pass through it we cross the sweet arrow a little river about eight mile from the susquehannah we cross it in our wagon mr jeremiah rees is our landlord his wife is sick with a fever arise from the hives at first he has a sister who seem to take the direction of the female part of the business
