one of the fellow calls himself james dickison he is one of their chief and a sensible fierce looking fellow but his character is very bad they say he has plunder several boat and murder many people that have been go down this river my curiosity lead me to visit them as they had all the appearance of friendship they eat and drink and smoke the calumet as it is good to have friend at court i carry the squaw some bread and as her infant was expose to the sun i give her my handkerchief to shade it for which she seem very thank mr mcfarlan give them some flour and meat and a bottle of whiskey to make glad come after honor us with their company for about an hour they wish us well and leave us in great good humor we arrive at the big cave
