dinner and at six o clock p m arrive at put up at steels tavern a very good tea i went to visit my old friend mrs james who receive me with her usual kind she me to accept of a bed but as we intend to se early in the morning i decline accept her kind offer arise very early with an inten to set of before breakfast but it set in to snow fast which detain us til ten o clock we ride before we bait our horse the road beyond description bad we could get no further that day than elizabeth town which is eighteen mile from lancaster had very good entertainment at the sign of the bear leave it before break the weather moderate a little but very rough road cross a beauty creek about a mile before town call swatana it tak horse go
