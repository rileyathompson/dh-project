tired so good night we come but nine or ten mile today and are now near the sixth mountain in a tavern fill with half drunk noisy wagoner one of them lie sing direct before the fire propose just now to call for a song from the young lady i can neither think nor write he make so much noise with his love song i am every moment expect something dreadful and dare not lay down my pen lest they should think me listen to them they are the very worst wretch that ever live i do believe i am out of all patience with them the whole world nor anything in it would tempt me to stay in this state three month i dislike everything belong to it i am not so foolish as to suppose there are no better people in it than those we have seen but
