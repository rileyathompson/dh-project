miss stare awhile drew a chair bid me sit and then run upstairs and put on two or three ring or else i had not seen them before and return set herself just before me show the way to reding that i may see her ornament perhaps to gain the more respect but her granam s new rung sow had it appear would affect me as much i pay honest john with money and dram according to contract and dismiss him and pray miss to show me where i must lodge she conduct me to a parlor in a little back lento which was almost fill with the bedstead which was so high that i was force to climb on a chair to get up to the wretch bed that lay on it on which have stretch my tired limb and lay my head on a sad color pillow
