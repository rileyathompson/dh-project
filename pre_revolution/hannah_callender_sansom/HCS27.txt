we see the beach we had that morning been on and they look out here for ship in fine it was worth see anna pole and hallender leave there name there in company with abundance more when we come to the road we part with all the company except those we come from newyork with tommy franklin polly morris tommy burling anna pole tommy pryor polly burling walter franklin and hallender ride to the halfway house and drink tea from thence please to newyork ferry from whence there is a good view of newyork from the south part and the ship diverse country seat on long island and above newyork one build by a brewer entire from the profit arise from east we land safe about seven o clock and all our friend well there is beacon place on a hill to alarm the country in case of an invasion
